EMPLOYEE POINTS & REWARDS PROGRAM — PARTICIPATION AGREEMENT

By registering on PointsParty HQ, I, the undersigned employee ("Participant"), agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW
The Employee Rewards Program ("Program") is administered by the Company and is designed to incentivize professional conduct, attendance, and performance through a points-based reward system. Participation is voluntary but subject to management approval.

2. POINT ACCUMULATION
Each approved Participant shall receive five hundred (500) points at the beginning of every calendar month, credited automatically to their account. Points do not have monetary value and cannot be transferred, sold, or redeemed for cash under any circumstances.

3. VIOLATIONS & DEDUCTIONS
The Company reserves the right to deduct points from any Participant's balance in the event of violations of company policy, code of conduct, or workplace regulations. Deductions shall be applied as follows: Mini Tiny Violation (−50 pts), Not So Mini Violation (−100 pts), Grande Violation (−200 pts), or as determined by the Admin for custom cases. All deductions shall include a written reason.

4. REWARD REDEMPTION
Rewards are unlocked upon reaching specified point milestones. Reward trips are organized by the Company and are subject to scheduling, availability, and administrative approval. The Company reserves the right to modify or cancel trips with reasonable notice.

5. ACCOUNT RESPONSIBILITY
Participants are solely responsible for maintaining the security of their login credentials. Sharing accounts is strictly prohibited and may result in account suspension.

6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes regarding point deductions must be raised with HR in writing within seven (7) business days of the deduction notice.

7. PROGRAM MODIFICATIONS
The Company reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate the Program at any time, with or without notice. Accumulated points may be adjusted accordingly.

8. CONSENT
By creating an account on this platform, I confirm that I have read, understood, and agreed to all terms stated in this Agreement. I understand that my account is subject to Admin approval before I can access the Program.
